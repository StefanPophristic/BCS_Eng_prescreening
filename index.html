Legal Information:
We invite you to participate in a research study on language production and comprehension.
Your experimenter will ask you to do a linguistic task such as reading sentences or words, naming pictures or describing scenes, making up sentences of your own, or participating in a simple language game.
You will be paid for your participation at the posted rate.
There are no risks or benefits of any kind involved in this study. If you have read this form and have decided to participate in this experiment, please understand your participation is voluntary and you have the right to withdraw your consent or discontinue participation at any time without penalty or loss of benefits to which you are otherwise entitled. You have the right to refuse to do particular tasks. Your individual privacy will be maintained in all published and written data resulting from the study. You may print this form for your records.

During the study, you will play an online guessing game with another person. You will see pictures and will use a chat box to send messages and identify objects. The game lasts about 30 min.

Since we are studying communication between people who know eachother, this study requires you to find a partner to play the game with you. If you choose to participate, find a partner and play the game at any time within a week of receiving the study link.

When we receive your results, you will be paid $7 through the Prolific Bonus Payment. Since your partner may not have prolific, your partner will receive $7 through an Amazon gift card.
